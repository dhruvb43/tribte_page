SARDAR PATEL

THE IRON MAN

__Here's a time line of sardar patel's life:__

  • BORN: 31 October 1875 - Vallabhbhai Jhaverbhai Patel,Patel was born into a self-sufficient ;andowing family of the leva patidar castle Nadiad, Bombay Presidency, British India
  • 1890: Schooling - Born in Nadiad village of modern day Gujarat to Zaverbhai and Ladbai on 31st October, 1875, Patel spent his early years in Karamsad.
  • 1897: Completed matriculation - Passed matriculation in Nadiad at the age of 22.
  • 1900 - set up an independent office of district pleader in godhra,two years later he moved to borsad .
  • 1918: Meeting Gandhi a turning point - Meeting Gandhi was a turning point in the Patel’s life. Gandhi chose Vallabhbhai as his Deputy Commander.
  • 1923: Elected President of Ahmedabad Municipality -Elected as the President of the Ahmedabad Municipal Board, Patel took charge and revamped the drainage, sanitation, cleanliness and water distribution systems of Ahmedabad.
  • 1927: Earned the title ‘Sardar’ -Leading a Peasant’s protest, Patel turned the trajectory of his life toward a path of public service. He led a mass No-Tax Campaign in Kheda District after which, peasants started to call him ‘Sardar’
